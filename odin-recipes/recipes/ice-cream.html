Ice Cream

Description

Ice cream is a creamy and delicious frozen dessert enjoyed by people of all ages. This homemade version allows you to customize the flavor and texture to your liking, creating a decadent treat that's perfect for any occasion.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Pinch of salt
  • Ice
  • Rock salt (for ice cream maker)

Steps

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine the heavy cream, whole milk, granulated sugar, vanilla extract, and a pinch of salt. Whisk together until the sugar is dissolved and the mixture is smooth.
  2. Cover the mixing bowl with plastic wrap or a lid and place it in the refrigerator to chill for at least 2 hours, or until the mixture is thoroughly chilled.
  3. If you're using an electric ice cream maker, follow the manufacturer's instructions to assemble and pre-chill the machine. If you're using a manual ice cream maker, make sure the inner container is frozen according to the manufacturer's guidelines.
  4. Once the ice cream mixture is chilled, pour it into the ice cream maker's freezer bowl.
  5. Turn on the ice cream maker and let it churn the mixture according to the manufacturer's instructions. This typically takes about 20-30 minutes, or until the ice cream reaches a soft-serve consistency.
  6. If you'd like to add any mix-ins, such as chocolate chips, chopped nuts, or fruit, you can gently fold them into the partially churned ice cream during the last few minutes of churning.
  7. Once the ice cream reaches the desired consistency, turn off the ice cream maker and transfer the ice cream to a freezer-safe container with a tight-fitting lid.
  8. Place the container of ice cream in the freezer and let it freeze for at least 4 hours, or until it's firm enough to scoop.
  9. When you're ready to enjoy the ice cream, remove it from the freezer and let it sit at room temperature for a few minutes to soften slightly.
  10. Enjoy!
